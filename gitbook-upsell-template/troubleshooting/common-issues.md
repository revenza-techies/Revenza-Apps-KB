# Troubleshoot common issues

Start with these quick checks before changing your offer.

## The offer is not visible

1. Confirm the offer is published.
2. Check that the current product matches the trigger rule.
3. Confirm the app embed or app block is enabled in the active theme.
4. Verify recommended products are active and available to the Online Store.
5. Test in a private browser window to rule out cached theme assets.

## A recommended product cannot be added

Check product status, variant availability, inventory, and sales-channel publishing.

## Still need help?

Email [support@revenza.in](mailto:support@revenza.in) with the store URL, affected product URL, and a screenshot of the issue.
