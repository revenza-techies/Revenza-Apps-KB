# Activate

Enable app embed to integrate the app with your live theme.

## Steps

1. Open Shopify admin.
2. Go to **Online Store** → **Themes**.
3. Select **Customize** on the live theme.
4. Open **App embeds**.
5. Enable **Revenza Upsell**.
6. Save the theme changes.

After activation, Revenza Upsell can display its app block on your storefront.
