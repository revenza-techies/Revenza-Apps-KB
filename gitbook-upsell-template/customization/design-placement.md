# Design and placement

Customize the offer so it feels like a natural part of your storefront.

## Placement

Choose a location that supports the customer's current task without obscuring product details or the main purchase action.

## Visual settings

Use your theme's type, button, and color choices as a starting point. Keep offer text concise and check contrast in both light and dark storefront sections.

## Responsive preview

Review the offer at desktop, tablet, and mobile widths before publishing.
