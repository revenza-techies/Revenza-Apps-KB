# Offer rules

Rules keep recommendations relevant by controlling which products trigger an offer and when it is visible.

## Choose trigger products

Select the products or collections that should display the offer.

## Review availability

Confirm every recommended product is active, available to the Online Store sales channel, and in stock when inventory tracking is enabled.

## Publish carefully

Preview changes before publishing and recheck the offer after changing product availability or theme settings.
