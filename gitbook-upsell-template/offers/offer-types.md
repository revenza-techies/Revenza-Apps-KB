# Offer types

Use the offer type that best fits the shopper's current decision.

## Product recommendation

Recommend a related or complementary product alongside the item a customer is viewing.

## Frequently bought together

Group products that make sense as a set and let customers add them with fewer steps.

## Add-on offer

Offer a useful extra, such as an accessory, service, or lower-priced companion product.
