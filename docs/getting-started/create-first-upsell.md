# Create your first upsell

An upsell connects a trigger product with one or more relevant recommendations.

## Create the offer

1. From Revenza Upsell, select **Create offer**.
2. Choose the product that should trigger the recommendation.
3. Add the products you want to recommend.
4. Choose the placement and display style.
5. Review the offer text and call to action.
6. Preview the experience, then select **Publish**.

## Check the storefront

Open the trigger product in your storefront and confirm the offer appears in the expected position. Test the add-to-cart flow on desktop and mobile.
