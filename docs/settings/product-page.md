# Product Page Settings

#### Enable Product Page Widget

Turn the product page upsell widget on or off.

* **Enabled:** Displays upsell products on the product page.
* **Disabled:** Hides the widget from the product page.

> **Note:** The widget must also be integrated into your theme using the App Block or App Embed.

***

#### Heading

Enter the title displayed above the upsell products.

**Example:** _Also Buy_, _You May Also Like_, _Recommended for You_.

***

#### Default Upsell Set

Choose the default upsell set to display on product pages.

If no custom mapping exists for a product, collection, or product tag, the selected default set will be used automatically.

> **Note:** Mapped upsell sets always take priority over the default set.

***

#### Product Count

Select the number of products to display in the upsell widget.

* Minimum: **1**
* Maximum: **20**

Choose a count that best fits your store's layout and customer experience.

***

#### Card Style

Select the visual design of the product cards displayed in the widget.

Different card styles allow you to match the upsell widget with your storefront's design.

***

#### Card Display Type

Choose how the product cards are arranged on the page.

Available layouts include responsive grid and slider options, depending on your preference and screen size.

***

#### Add to Cart Button

Choose how customers can add upsell products to their cart.

Available options determine whether upsell products are added using the store's primary **Add to Cart** button or a separate action within the widget.
