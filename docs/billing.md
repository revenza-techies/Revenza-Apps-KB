# Billing

Billing information for Revenza Upsell is managed through Shopify.

## What to check

- Confirm the app plan connected to your Shopify store.
- Review any plan limits before creating more upsell sets.
- Check Shopify billing for invoices or subscription changes.

For billing questions, contact Revenza support with your store URL and app name.
