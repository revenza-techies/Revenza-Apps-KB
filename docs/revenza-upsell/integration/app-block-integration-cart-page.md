# App Block Integration - Cart Page

<Stepper>
  <Step>
    ### Open the Theme Customizer

    Go to: **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Open the Cart Template

    From the page selector at the top, choose: **Cart**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Add the App Block

    * Click **+ Add section** (or **+ Add block**, depending on your theme).
    * Select **Apps**.
    * Choose **Revenza Upsell block**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Position the Block

    Move the app block to your preferred location and click **Save.**
  </Step>
</Stepper>

**Result:** The configured addon products will be displayed on the cart page, allowing customers to add more items before proceeding to checkout.
