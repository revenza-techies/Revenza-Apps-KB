# App Block Integration - Product Page

<Stepper>
  <Step>
    ### Open the Theme Customizer

    In your Shopify admin, navigate to:\
    **Online Store → Themes → Customize**
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Open the Product Template

    From the page selector at the top, choose:\
    **Products → Default Product**

    _If your store uses a custom product template, select that template instead._
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Add the App Block

    * Click **+ Add block** where you want the upsell widget to appear.
    * Select **Apps**.
    * Choose **Revenza Upsell**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Position the Block

    Drag and drop the app block to your preferred location on the product page.\
    Click **Save** to publish your changes.
  </Step>
</Stepper>

**Result:** The upsell products configured for the current product will be displayed in the selected location.
