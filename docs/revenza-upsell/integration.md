# Integration

## App Block (2.0) - Integration

Use the **App Block** to display upsell products on your **Product** and **Cart** pages without editing your theme code.

This no-code integration method allows you to quickly add upsell widgets to your storefront, making it easier for customers to discover relevant products and encouraging additional purchases.

## Product Page Integration

#### Step 1: Open the Theme Customizer

In your Shopify admin, navigate to:

**Online Store → Themes → Customize**

#### Step 2: Open the Product Template

From the page selector at the top, choose:

**Products → Default Product**

:::info
_If your store uses a custom product template, select that template instead._
:::

#### Step 3: Add the App Block

1. Click **+ Add block** where you want the upsell widget to appear.
2. Select **Apps**.
3. Choose **Easy Product Addons**.

#### Step 4: Position the Block

Drag and drop the app block to your preferred location on the product page.

Click **Save** to publish your changes.

> **Result:** The addon products configured for the current product will be displayed in the selected location.

---

## Cart Page Integration

#### Step 1: Open the Theme Customizer

Go to:

**Online Store → Themes → Customize**

#### Step 2: Open the Cart Template

From the page selector at the top, choose:

**Cart**

#### Step 3: Add the App Block

1. Click **+ Add section** (or **+ Add block**, depending on your theme).
2. Select **Apps**.
3. Choose **Easy Product Addons**.

#### Step 4: Position the Block

Move the app block to your preferred location on the cart page.

Click **Save** to apply the changes.

> **Result:** The configured addon products will be displayed on the cart page, allowing customers to add more items before proceeding to checkout.
