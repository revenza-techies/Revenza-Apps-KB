# Getting Started

The setup takes less than a minute.

<Stepper>
  <Step>
    ### Open Theme Editor

    Open the **Revenza Upsell** app from **Apps**, then click **Activate** in the **Enable App Embed** section.

    _**Note:** The button opens your currently published Shopify theme in the Theme Editor._
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Save the App Embed

    In the Theme Editor, click **Save** in the top-right corner to enable the app embed on your live theme.

    _**Important:** The app embed is not activated until you save the theme._
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Verify Activation

    Return to the **Revenza Upsell** app and confirm that the **App Embed** status shows **Enabled**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Preview Upsells

    Click [**Preview**](/docs/revenza-upsell/getting-started/upsell-product-preview) in the **Product Page** section to view the default **Pre-built Upsells** on your product pages.
  </Step>
</Stepper>
